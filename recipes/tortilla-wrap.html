Tortilla Wrap

Description

Tortilla wraps are a quick, versatile meal inspired by Mexican cuisine. Soft flatbreads filled with beans, vegetables, cheese, or meat make them flavorful and satisfying.

They are easy to customize and ideal for lunches or fast dinners. Fresh salsa, avocado, or extra protein can be added for more energy.

Ingredients

  • Flour tortilla
  • 3-4 tbsp refried or black beans (~50g)
  • 2 tbsp shredded cheese
  • 2 tbsp salsa
  • Optional: ~30g of avocado, sour cream, cooked chicken or beef
  • Optional: 3-4 slices of tomato and cucumber, a handful of greens.

Steps

  1. Warm the tortilla in a pan (10 sec. each side).
  2. Spread beans evenly in the center.
  3. Sprinkle cheese on top of beans.
  4. Layer tomato, cucumber, greens and optional meat.
  5. Spoon salsa over fillings.
  6. Fold in the sides and roll tightly.
  7. Toast the rolled wrap in the pan for 20-30 seconds per side until lightly golden. Serve warm.
