Oats and Berries

Description

Oats with berries is a quick, nourishing breakfast. Reach in fiber, naturally sweet and full of vitamins, they are healthy and delicious.

This easy and customizable meal is perfect for busy mornings. Some people add protein powder into the mix for extra nutrition and longer-lasting energy.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup oats
  • 1 cup milk or water
  • A handful of berries of choice (blueberry, cranberry, blackberry etc.)
  • (Optional): A handful of fruit of choice (mango, banana, apple etc.)
  • (Optional): 1 scoop protein powder
  • (Optional): 1 tbsp peanut butter

Steps

  1. Microwave oats for 1-2 min.
  2. (Optional): stir in the protein scoop.
  3. Add berries.
  4. (Optional): chop and then add fruit of choice.
  5. Add chia seeds or other fillings of choice, if you wish.
